About Leafbarrow

The Leafbarrow is the result of years of engineering and product development. It enables faster and larger yard cleanups for leaves, mulch, branches and more.

No need to rake leaves into a pile, you can move the Leafbarrow directly up to a leaf pile and efficiently rake directly into it.

Pick up leaves, grass, gravel, and dirt as you go, the pile is made inside the barrow, and you can move it around your yard easily.

Anything a wheel barrow can hold, the Leafbarrow can hold, plus more.
