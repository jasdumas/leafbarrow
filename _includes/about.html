About Leafbarrow

The Leafbarrow is a revolutionary landscaping product that helps with all sorts of cleanup jobs around your yard. It enables faster and larger yard cleanups for leaves, mulch, branches, grass, gravel and more. It can also be used to transport heavier objects around without the need for bulky tarps or wobbly wheel barrows. The Leafbarrow has been a labor of love; the result of years of engineering and product development, making it the ideal companion for anyone looking to care for their yard quickly and more efficiently.

How it Works

No need to rake leaves into a pile! As you rake leaves, move the Leafbarrow directly to the spot that needs clearing and rake directly into it, creating the pile inside the barrow. When you are finished and need to dump the pile, the Leafbarrow lifts to an intermediate position off the ground, making it easier to move. The rear bucket can be removed to easily empty the Leafbarrow out into a dump pile for composting.

Innovative Design

The Leafbarrow is expertly designed to make your job easier:

  • Rake leaves up the front ramp directly from the ground into the barrow.
  • Use the levered handle to bring the barrow into position for easy transportation.
  • The rear bucket is removable for easy dumping.
